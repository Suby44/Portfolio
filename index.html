Formula SAE — Suspension Rockers (Front & Rear)

Designed, analyzed, and manufactured front and rear suspension rockers for Queen's Formula SAE car.

  • Designed suspension rockers to convert wheel travel into controlled damper motion, optimizing motion ratio and suspension response
  • Modeled rocker geometry in SolidWorks, iterating pivot locations and profiles to balance packaging constraints and load paths
  • Performed FEA on critical load cases (bump, landing, cornering) to verify strength and stiffness, achieving an adequate safety factor
  • Manufactured rockers using waterjet cutting and CNC milling, generating custom Mastercam toolpaths and shop drawings
  • Will validate final parts through physical fit-up and assembly on the vehicle, confirming alignment, smooth rotation, and structural integrity
